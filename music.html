Music Projects


I love music and it has been an important part of my life since before I can remember. My main instruments are the guitar, drums, bass, and ukelele. I have recently come into learning music production, and I am taking piano lessons on the Grinnell campus. My passion for music comes not only from my joy of listening, but the fresh and unique experience of playing with others. Our on campus group, Freesound, fosters this sentiment by providing means for students to meet and perform. My goal in music is to play, write, and perform as much as I can.





Production
